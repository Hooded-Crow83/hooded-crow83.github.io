Augusta Blind Sports Association

Helping visually impaired people all over Georgia discover their athletic potential.

Donate Now

Founded in 2011, the Georgia Blind Sports Association is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to advocating for visually impaired athletes of all ages in Georgia. We encourage these individuals to engage in sports that foster teamwork, promote a healthy lifestyle, and empower them to fully utilize their athletic talents. To support our mission and help us continue providing these valuable opportunities, please consider making a donation. Your contributions go directly towards purchasing equipment, renting facilities, providing accommodations, and increasing awareness. Your donation contributes to:

  • Purchasing Equipment
  • Registration Fees
  • Expand Programs
  • Travel Expenses
  • Creating Opportunities
  • Enriching Lives
