About Us

Founded in 2011, the Georgia Blind Sports Association is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to advocating for visually impaired athletes of all ages in Georgia. We encourage these individuals to engage in sports that foster teamwork, promote a healthy lifestyle, and empower them to fully utilize their athletic talents.
